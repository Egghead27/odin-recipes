Aglio e Olio

Description

The perfect late night dish for when you're feeling hungy in the middle of the night. This simple pasta dish is easy to make with simple ingredients and taste absolutely wonderful. Let's make Aglio e Olio.

Ingredients

These are the ingredients you'll need:

  • 1 pound uncooked spaghetti
  • 1/2 cup of olive oil
