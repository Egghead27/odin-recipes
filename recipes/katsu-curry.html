Japanese Katsu Curry

Description

There's nothing more comforting than eating japanese katsu curry. This simple meal will always be a staple in my household and I'll always make it whenever I want to go back to comforts. And today, I'll show you how to make it too.

Ingredients

These are the ingredients for the curry (roughly 4 servings).

  • 2 blocks of Golden Curry (whichever spice level you want)
  • 2 Potatoes
  • 2 Carrots
  • Half an Onion
  • 4 cups of Water
  • Salt & White Pepper

These are the ingredients for 1 serving of katsu to go along with the curry

  • 1 Chicken Breast
  • 1 cup of Flour
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 cup of Panko Breadcrumbs
  • Frying oil
  • Salt & Black Pepper

Steps

These are the steps to make a delicious Katsu Curry

  1. Cut the onion into half moons. Put some frying oil and a little bit of water into the pot and caramelize the onion in the pot for about 15-20 minutes. Season with salt.
  2. Cut the carrots and potatoes into small chunks.
  3. After the onion has browned and caramelized, put in the carrots and potatoes to fry until glistening and coated with the oil, usually about 10 minutes.
  4. Put the 4 cups of water in the pot and leave it all to simmer aggressively for 30 minutes. Season with salt and white pepper to taste.

Enjoy!!
