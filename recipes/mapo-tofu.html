Mapo Tofu

Description

Nothing that warm fuzzy feeling after you've eaten a good Mapo Tofu. In today's recipe, I will show you how to make a steaming hot Mapo Tofu to soothe your soul.

Ingredients

These are the ingredients you'll need:

  • 1 Package of soft tofu (usually around 400 gr)
  • 120 gr of ground chicken.
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil.
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced.
  • 1 green onion.
  • 3 red chili (to taste).
  • 1 1/2 tbsp of Doubanjian (chili bean paste)
  • 1 Cup of chicken stock or water.
  • 1 tbsp of oyster sauce.
  • 1/2 tsp each of sugar and ground sichuan pepper.
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch.
  • 1 tsp sesame oil.

Steps

These are the steps on how to make a Mapo Tofu

  1. Heat oil in a wok over medium heat. Add garlic, white parts of green onion, and chopped chili and cook for 30 seconds or until fragrant. Add minced meat and cook for about 2 minutes while breaking up the meat with a spatula.
  2. Add doubanjiang and fry for another minute, stirring well to combine. Add chicken stock (or water) and season with oyster sauce, dark soy sauce, sugar, and ground sichuan pepper. Stir to combine and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add cornstarch slurry and stir continuously until thickened. Carefully stir in your tofu. Cook for about 2 minutes until the tofu is heated through. Season with salt to taste if needed, and finish with sesame oil.
  4. Transfer to a bowl, sprinkle with reserved green onions, and serve immediately over a bowl of steamed rice.

Enjoy!!
